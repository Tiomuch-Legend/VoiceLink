# VoiceLink

**VoiceLink – Your hands-free PC assistant**  
VoiceLink is a desktop application that allows you to control your computer using voice commands. It supports mouse control, typing, AI-powered assistant interactions, and other actions without touching your keyboard or mouse, making your workflow faster, more accessible, and fully contactless.

## About

VoiceLink combines computer vision, speech recognition, and AI to provide seamless, hands-free control of your PC.  
It is ideal for users who want to increase productivity, make computing accessible for people with limited mobility, or experiment with voice-controlled applications.

Key technologies: **Python, OpenCV, Mediapipe, PyAudio, PyAutoGUI, speech recognition libraries, and AI assistant integration.**

---

## Features

- **Mouse control** – Move the mouse with gestures and voice commands.  
- **Typing control** – Type hands-free using voice commands.  
- **AI assistant** – Ask questions, get answers, and interact with your computer using AI-powered voice responses.  
- **Command recognition** – Recognizes a set of predefined commands (start, stop, type, click, etc.).  
- **Real-time feedback** – Shows visual cues while listening and performing actions.
